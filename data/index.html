About

Smoothlife is an generalization of Conway's Game of Life, expanded to use real values. The original design and implementation is by Stephan Rafler. You can find his paper at http://arxiv.org/pdf/1111.1567v2.pdf. The original source can be found at http://sourceforge.net/projects/smoothlife.

The source for the NaCl implementation can be found at http://github.com/binji/smoothnacl.
